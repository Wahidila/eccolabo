## The Positioning

**From workshop thinking to industrial execution.**

Over the past seven years, ECOLLABO8 has evolved from a small experimental recycling workshop into a mature industrial facility processing more than 5 tons of plastic per day.

**The biggest lesson we learned:**

> Recycling only works when it doesn't need the word "recycling" to sell.

The turning point was not a new machine. It was a **business-first approach**:

- Market before machinery
- Demand before production
- Profit before impact

This masterclass is built on that exact framework.
